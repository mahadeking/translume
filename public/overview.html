What is Translume?

It's a screen & camera recorder that lives in your browser. You hit record, talk through whatever's on your screen, and the moment you stop you get a shareable link. The person you send it to can watch it any time and leave comments pinned to the exact moment in the video — so a five-minute explanation replaces a long email or a scheduled call. Think of it as a friendlier, more modern take on async video at work.

How it works

Four simple steps — most messages take under a minute.

1

Record

Capture your screen, your camera, or both. A little webcam bubble follows along, and a 3‑2‑1 countdown gets you ready.

2

Get an instant link

Stop recording and your video is ready immediately — no upload wait. Copy the link or embed it anywhere.

3

People reply on the video

Viewers leave comments and emoji reactions pinned to the exact second, so feedback is crystal clear.

4

AI does the busywork

Translume writes a title, a summary, and chapter markers automatically — plus a full transcript and captions.
